About Us

We are a young company founded in 2021 and focusing on new technology development for Electric Vehicle Charging Services.

We are motivated to build a strong technical team who can quickly adapt to fast growing technologies. The team has solid background in electrical engineering, especially in power electronics, electric drives and industrial communications.

  • 3 Full-time senior researchers
  • 6 Researchers
  • 3 Back-office staff
  • 30 Interns

We develop EVSE device, DC Quick Charging station, Private charger, Onboard charger. We provide Pre-compliant test, Technology transfer, Reversed engineering services. We offer Workforce education and training.

Technology company in the field of EVSE

  • Developing prototypes of EV Level 2, Level 3 DC chargers and eBike Chargers within the first 3 years
  • Achieving First Version of Products, Certificates and Patents. Conducting field test and workforce education and training within 5 years.
  • Mass production of EV Level 2 charging poles, EV Level 3 charging stations, and eBike private chargers. Providing high quality installation and maintenance services

Continuous development and strong motivation

Constant learning, Reliable delivery and Work with heart.

Ready for serving the world

Reduction of gap between research and mass production; Increment of Electric vehicle charging technology readiness; Minimizing electric vehicle charging technology development time.
